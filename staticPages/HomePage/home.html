Dolphin Rescue | Found In 2001

Dolphin Rescue is a non-profit organization based on the purpuse of saving the ocean by selling products that will contribute to the deep cleaning of ocean pollution. Our company is mainly focused on dolphins due to the fact that the death rate of these creature increase by 20% every year. However, as our main goal is to clean the ocean, every living creature in it, is benefited.

Dolphin Health Institution

Whithin our organization, we have a program called, Dolphin Health Institution. In this program we have Doctors specialized to treat dolphins that are found injured near the coast. We also count with the help of the best dolphin trainers in our area to help these creatures to develop their muscles. The Dolphin Health Institution is dedicated to work hard to also find solutions for the increasing of global warming, ocean pollution, and many other environmental changes.
